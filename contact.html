About Us

Established since 2013, Goldent Mutlispeciality Dental Hospital & Implant Centre in Beeramguda, Hyderabad is a top player in the category Dental Hospitals in the Hyderabad. This well-known establishment acts as a one-stop destination servicing patients both local and from other parts of Hyderabad. Over the course of its journey, this hospital has established a firm foothold in dentistry. The belief that patients satisfaction is as important as their products and services, have helped this establishment garner a vast base of patients, which continues to grow by the day.
